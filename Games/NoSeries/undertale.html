Having trouble with keyboard controls not inputting?

Put your mouse cursor to the side of the game player (or scroll bar), Scroll down, Find a black box with thick white borders, Click on it, then click the game, Then scroll back up. You should be able now to correctly input keyboard keys into the game player.
